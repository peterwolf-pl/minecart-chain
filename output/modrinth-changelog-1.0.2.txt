Minecart Chain 1.0.2

Added
- Added a second furnace minecart control lever: Throttle.
- The throttle toggles between slow power (1/3 power) and full power.
- Added colored control pads on furnace minecarts: red for Brake, black for Throttle.

Changed
- Reduced the target spacing between linked minecarts by 0.5 blocks.
- Improved corner handling so linked carts apply less resistance while turning.
- Slow throttle mode now limits engine force and speed for smoother low-speed driving.
- Full throttle mode restores maximum engine power for faster travel.

Notes
- Brake remains engaged by default when a furnace minecart is first linked.
- Click the brake side of the furnace minecart to toggle the brake.
- Click the throttle side of the furnace minecart to switch between slow and full power.
