Minecart Chain 1.0.6

Added
- Added locomotive fueling with all vanilla furnace fuels, including coal, charcoal, logs, planks, sticks, wooden tools, and lava buckets.
- Added locomotive water filling with water buckets.

Changed
- Furnace minecart locomotives now require both fuel and water before the custom engine can drive.
- Fuel is consumed only while the locomotive is driving, and the fuel cost scales with the number of connected minecarts in the train.
- Water is consumed while driving at a slower train-size-scaled rate.
- Coal and charcoal keep the vanilla furnace minecart fuel value, while other fuels scale from vanilla furnace burn times.
